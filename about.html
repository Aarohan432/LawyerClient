About RAM RAGHVENDRA & ASSOCIATES

18+ Years of Legal Excellence | Trusted Advocate Since 2008

Advocate Ram Raghvendra Kumar

RAM RAGHVENDRA & ASSOCIATES is a premier law firm based in Rohini, Delhi, led by Advocate Ram Raghvendra Kumar. With over 18 years of continuous legal practice since 2008, the firm has established itself as a trusted name in the legal fraternity.

Advocate Ram Raghvendra Kumar completed his LL.B from Patna University and started his legal journey as a junior advocate at Tis Hazari Courts. His dedication, meticulous preparation, and persuasive advocacy quickly earned him recognition, leading to the establishment of his independent practice and later RAM RAGHVENDRA & ASSOCIATES.

The firm regularly appears before the District Courts of Delhi (Rohini, Tis Hazari, Saket, Patiala House) and the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India. Specialized expertise includes MACT, Consumer Protection, Civil Litigation, Labour Disputes, Arbitration, Criminal Defence, and Special Leave Petitions (SLPs).

What sets RAM RAGHVENDRA & ASSOCIATES apart is the client-centric approach, transparent communication, and unwavering commitment to justice. The firm believes in keeping clients informed at every stage and providing practical, cost-effective legal solutions.
